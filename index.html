Hackathons first emerged as a method of facilitating innovative thinking around 2010 primarily on university campuses.

Since then, hackathons have expanded rapidly to companies and non-profit organizations worldwide. Our team has advised over 50 organizations ranging from the MIT Media Lab to the Vatican City on how to organize their hackathons.

We are a collective of students from Harvard, MIT, and other schools in the Boston area. We have all organized (and often founded) hackathons before. Unlike other consulting or event-planning groups, hackathons are our specialty.

Wide variety of knowledge

Our team can help you plan many aspects of your hackathon event

Logistics

  • Venue selection and reservation
  • Venue electrical setup plan
  • Wi-fi provider contracting for event
  • Meal planning, delivery, and service
  • Participant swag sourcing and distribution

Participants

  • Application design & facilitation
  • Participant applications and selection
  • Support for participant questions
  • Participant travel coordination and travel reimbursement (if applicable)

Competition

  • Define competition rules, guidelines, and FAQ
  • Gather participant submissions and display in a gallery post-event
  • Facilitate judging and select winners
  • Award prizes and connect winners with future opportunities
